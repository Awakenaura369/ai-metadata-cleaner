What gets removed?

This tool re-renders your image through the browser's Canvas API, which effectively strips all EXIF metadata — including GPS coordinates, camera model, creation date, software information, and other embedded tags.

Note: the tool does not remove watermarks or perceptual AI detection patterns (like invisible digital watermarks embedded in pixel values), as those require server-side processing. It is best suited for removing standard image metadata tags.

Privacy Policy

Local Processing: All image processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

What we strip: EXIF tags, GPS coordinates, creation timestamps, camera info, and software metadata embedded in JPEG/PNG/WebP files.

Limitations: We cannot remove invisible perceptual watermarks or AI model-specific steganographic signatures embedded in pixel data. Those require specialized tools.

Ads: We display third-party advertisements. These vendors may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history.

No account required: We do not collect personal information, require sign-up, or track your uploaded files in any way.
